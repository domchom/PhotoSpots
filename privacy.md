PhotoSpots Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

PhotoSpots is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Data You Create
All photo spots, lists, notes, and photos you add are stored on your device and synced privately to your iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data is yours — it is never accessed by the developer or shared with third parties.

Location
PhotoSpots requests access to your device's location solely to show your position on the map and calculate distances to nearby spots. Your location is never transmitted off your device or stored.

Photos
Photos you attach to spots are stored locally and in your iCloud account. They are never uploaded to any server outside of Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

In-App Purchases
Tips are processed entirely by Apple. The developer does not collect or store any payment information.

Notifications
PhotoSpots may request notification permission for optional reminders. No notification data leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking
PhotoSpots collects no analytics, uses no third-party SDKs, and does no tracking of any kind.
